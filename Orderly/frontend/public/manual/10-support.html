Support

If you run into an issue that this manual does not cover, or if something in your Orderly dashboard is not behaving as expected, the Orderly support team is available to help.

Getting Support

✉️ Email

Send your inquiry to support@orderly.com. Include a description of the issue, what you were trying to do, what happened instead, and any error messages you saw. Screenshots are always helpful. You can expect a response within 24 business hours.

📞 Phone

Call us at 1-800-555-0418. Phone support is available Monday through Friday, from 8:00 am to 6:00 pm.

🏢 Headquarters

9101 Fayetteville Rd,
Raleigh, NC 27603.

Before reaching out, it is worth checking this manual first — most common questions about features and workflows are covered here. Use the chapter links in the sidebar to find the relevant section.
